Spicy Ginger & Garlic Fish Stir Fry

A light, aromatic dish that packs a punch of heat. Perfect for a quick weeknight dinner that doesn't compromise on nutrition.

Ingredients

  • • 1 lb white fish fillets (cod or tilapia)
  • • 2 tbsp low sodium soy sauce
  • • 1 tbsp fresh ginger, minced
  • • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • • 1 red chili or 1/2 tsp flakes
  • • 2 cups broccoli florets
  • • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • • 1 tbsp sesame oil & 1/4 cup water

Preparation Steps

  1. 01

    In a small bowl, whisk together the soy sauce, minced ginger, and minced garlic.

  2. 02

    Heat sesame oil in a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat. Sear fish pieces for 3-4 minutes until opaque; set aside.

  3. 03

    Toss broccoli, bell pepper, and chili into the pan. Stir fry for 5 minutes until tender-crisp.

  4. 04

    Return fish to pan, pour in the sauce mixture and water. Toss gently for 2 minutes until glazed and thickened.

Chef's Note: Serve as is for a high-protein, low-starch powerhouse meal.

Chili & Garlic Roasted Chicken

A rustic, one-pan wonder that balances lean protein with wholesome vegetables and savory herbs.

Ingredients

  • • 2 chicken breasts
  • • 2 small potatoes
  • • 2 cups zucchini
  • • 1 cup cherry tomatoes
  • • 4 cloves garlic
  • • 1 tsp chili flakes
  • • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • • Dried oregano

Instructions

  1. 1 Preheat oven to 400°F.
  2. 2 Combine chicken, potatoes, zucchini, and tomatoes in a roasting pan.
  3. 3 Add garlic, chili flakes, oregano, salt, and pepper. Drizzle with oil and toss.
  4. 4 Roast for 25-30 mins, turning halfway, until potatoes are tender.

Spicy Ginger & Garlic Beef Curry

Comfort in a bowl. Creamy light coconut milk meets bold red curry and tender lean beef.

Ingredients

  • • 1 lb lean beef
  • • 1 tbsp coconut oil
  • • 1 yellow onion
  • • 2 tbsp grated ginger
  • • 4 cloves garlic
  • • 2 tbsp red curry paste
  • • 14oz light coconut milk
  • • Spinach & Carrots

Instructions

  1. 1 Sauté onion in coconut oil, then add ginger, garlic, and green chili.
  2. 2 Brown beef slices, then stir in curry paste for 1 minute.
  3. 3 Add coconut milk and carrots. Simmer for 15 minutes until tender.
  4. 4 Stir in spinach at the end until wilted. Serve with minimal rice.
