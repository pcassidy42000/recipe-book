Bavarian Sandwich with a Tall Glass of Milk

Prep Time Not specified
Difficulty Easy
Serving 1 Person

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Butter (liberally applied)
  • Ham
  • Apple Slices
  • Cheddar Cheese
  • Mango Chutney (with added chilli)

Chef's Note

Serve with a tall glass of cold milk as specified to balance the sweet and spicy mango chutney.

Method

  1. The Foundation

    Liberally butter the bread slices. Ensure the butter reaches all the way to the edges for maximum richness.

  2. Assembly

    Assemble the sandwich by layering the ham, fresh apple slices, and sharp cheddar cheese. The combination of salty meat and crisp fruit is essential.

  3. The Secret Ingredient

    Spread the mango chutney with added chilli generously onto the sandwich. This provides a delightful heat that cuts through the rich cheddar.

  4. Service

    Close the sandwich and serve immediately. Don't forget that tall glass of milk!
